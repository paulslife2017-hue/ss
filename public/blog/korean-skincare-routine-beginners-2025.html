Why the Korean Skincare Routine Works

Korean skincare philosophy focuses on prevention rather than correction, hydration over everything, and consistency for results. The multi-step approach allows you to layer lightweight products that penetrate deeply, rather than applying one heavy cream that sits on top of your skin.

FAQ

Q: Do I really need all 10 steps?

A: No! Start with 3-5 steps (cleanse, moisturize, sunscreen). Add steps gradually as your skin adjusts. Many Koreans don't even do all 10 steps daily.

Q: How long does the routine take?

A: Morning: 5-10 minutes. Evening: 10-20 minutes (full 10-step). Beginners: 3-5 minutes each.

Q: Can I use Western products in my K-beauty routine?

A: Absolutely! Mix and match based on what works for your skin.
