What Happens During a Gangnam Head Spa? (Step-by-Step)

Step 1: Scalp Analysis (15 minutes)

Korean head spas use 200x magnification scalp cameras to analyze your scalp condition. You'll see your scalp on a screen showing:

  • Oil production levels
  • Clogged hair follicles
  • Dead skin buildup
  • Hair density and thickness

Step 2: Deep Cleansing (20 minutes)

Specialized scalp cleansers (often containing tea tree, peppermint, or herbal extracts) are applied and massaged in. Some clinics use ultrasonic devices to break down sebum plugs.

Step 3: Scalp Massage (30 minutes)

This is the highlight! Therapists use acupressure techniques targeting specific scalp points to:

  • Stimulate blood circulation
  • Release tension
  • Promote hair growth
  • Provide deep relaxation

Step 4: Treatment Application (10 minutes)

Custom scalp ampoules (serums) are applied based on your scalp type. Common ingredients:

  • Hair loss: Caffeine, biotin, ginseng
  • Oily scalp: Salicylic acid, tea tree
  • Dry scalp: Hyaluronic acid, ceramides

Step 5: LED Light Therapy (10 minutes)

Red or blue LED light therapy is applied to enhance treatment absorption and stimulate follicles.

Step 6: Hair Treatment (15 minutes)

A nourishing hair mask is applied, followed by steam therapy and gentle blow-drying.

FAQ - Gangnam Head Spa

Q: How often should I get a head spa?

A: For maintenance: once a month. For specific issues (hair loss, severe dandruff): every 2 weeks for 3 months, then monthly.

Q: Will it hurt?

A: No! It's deeply relaxing. Some pressure during massage but never painful. Most clients fall asleep during treatment.

Q: Can I wash my hair after?

A: Wait 24 hours to let treatment ampoules absorb. Your hair will be washed and styled at the clinic, so you'll leave looking great.

Q: Is it good for hair loss?

A: Yes! Regular head spa treatments can reduce hair shedding by 30-50%. For active hair loss, book the specialized Hair Loss Treatment (‚Ç©180,000).

Q: How long do results last?

A: Immediate effects (clean scalp, relaxation) last 7-10 days. Long-term benefits (reduced hair loss, improved hair quality) require 3-6 sessions.
