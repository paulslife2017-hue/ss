Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1. Is BB Glow painful?

A. With numbing cream, most clients rate pain as 2-3 out of 10. You'll feel slight pressure and tingling, but it's very tolerable.

Q2. How many sessions do I need?

A. For optimal results: 3-4 sessions spaced 2 weeks apart. Darker skin tones may need 4-5 sessions for full coverage.

Q3. Can I still wear makeup?

A. Yes! BB Glow provides a perfect base. You can apply makeup for special occasions, but most clients find they don't need it daily.

Q4. Will it look natural?

A. Absolutely! The color is customized to match your exact skin tone. Results look like naturally perfect skin, not a makeup layer.

Q5. What if I don't like it?

A. BB Glow naturally fades over 6-12 months as skin cells renew. If you want faster removal, laser treatments can accelerate fading.

Q6. Can I get BB Glow if I have acne scars?

A. Yes! BB Glow helps camouflage acne scars and evens out skin texture. However, wait until active breakouts have healed.
