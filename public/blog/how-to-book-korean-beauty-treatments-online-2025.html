Common Booking Questions Answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to pay a deposit when booking?

A: Most clinics on KBeautySeoul don't require deposits. You pay at the clinic after your treatment. However, some high-end clinics may require a small deposit (10-20%) to secure your booking.

Q: What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

A: Cancellation policies vary by clinic. Generally, free cancellation is allowed up to 24 hours before appointment. Late cancellations may incur fees. Always check the specific clinic's policy before booking.

Q: Can I book same-day appointments?

A: Yes! Many clinics accept same-day bookings through KBeautySeoul platform. However, availability may be limited during peak hours (11am-3pm).

Q: Is English spoken at the clinics?

A: All clinics listed on KBeautySeoul platform have English-speaking staff or provide translation services.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?

A: Most clinics accept cash, credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and sometimes mobile payment apps (KakaoPay, NaverPay).
