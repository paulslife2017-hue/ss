‚ùì FAQ - Beauty Tourism in Seoul

Q: Do I need a visa for beauty tourism in Korea?

A: Citizens of 112 countries (including US, UK, Canada, EU, Australia, Japan) get 90-day visa-free entry. Check Korean Immigration for your country.

Q: Can I get same-day beauty treatments?

A: Possible but not recommended. Popular treatments (head spa, BB glow) book up 1-2 weeks in advance. Book ahead via kbeautyseoul.co.kr.

Q: Are Seoul beauty treatments safe for foreigners?

A: Very safe! Korea has strict regulations. All clinics on K-Beauty Seoul are licensed, insured, and have foreign customer experience.

Q: What's the best time of year to visit for beauty tourism?

A: September-November (fall) - pleasant weather, beauty deals, fewer Chinese tourists than summer. Avoid: July-August (hot, rainy, crowded).

Q: Can I bring beauty products back home?

A: Yes! Most products allowed. Skincare under 100ml can go in carry-on. Check customs limits for sheet masks (some countries restrict agricultural products).

Q: Do clinics offer package deals for tourists?

A: Yes! Many clinics have "beauty tour packages" (3-5 treatments bundled at 20-30% discount). Best booked through K-Beauty Seoul.
