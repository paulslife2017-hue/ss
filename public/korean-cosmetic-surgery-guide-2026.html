üíâ Most Popular Korean Cosmetic Surgery Procedures

1. Double Eyelid Surgery (Blepharoplasty)

Cost Range: $1,500 - $3,500

Recovery Time: 7-10 days

Description: Creates a defined eyelid crease for those born without one. The most popular cosmetic procedure in Korea, accounting for 30% of all surgeries.

Techniques:

  • Non-incision method (suture method) - minimal scarring, 5-7 days recovery
  • Partial incision method - semi-permanent results

Best Clinics: ID Hospital, BK Plastic Surgery, ITEM Plastic Surgery

Cost Range: $3,000 - $8,000

Recovery Time: 2-3 weeks

Description: Korean rhinoplasty focuses on creating natural, harmonious facial proportions rather than dramatic changes.

Common Procedures:

  • Tip refinement
  • Nostril reduction
  • Deviated septum correction

Best Clinics: BANOBAGI, The Plus Plastic Surgery, Dream Medical Group

3. Facial Contouring Surgery (V-Line Surgery)

  2. Days 1-7: Acute Recovery
    • Follow post-op care instructions carefully
    • Take prescribed medications

  4. Weeks 3-4: Extended Recovery

    • Final check-up before departure
    • Get post-op care instructions for home

  1. Month 1: Continue gentle care, avoid strenuous activity
  2. Months 2-3: Gradual return to normal activities

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long should I plan to stay in Korea for cosmetic surgery?

A: It depends on the procedure:

  • Minor procedures (double eyelid, fillers): 10-14 days
  • Moderate procedures (rhinoplasty, breast): 2-3 weeks
  • Major procedures (V-line, facial contouring): 3-4 weeks minimum

Always add extra time for unexpected swelling or complications.

Q: Do I need to speak Korean?

A: Not necessarily. Major clinics in Gangnam have English-speaking coordinators and some surgeons speak English. However, learning basic Korean phrases is helpful for daily life during recovery.

Q: Can I combine multiple procedures?

A: Yes, many patients combine procedures to save time and money. Common combinations include:

  • Double eyelid + nose surgery
  • Facial contouring + chin augmentation
  • Breast augmentation + liposuction

However, recovery time increases proportionally.

Q: What if I'm not happy with the results?

A: Most reputable clinics offer:

  • Revision surgery - Sometimes free within certain timeframe (check contract)
  • Refund policy - Rare, usually only for severe complications
  • Mediation services - Korea Medical Dispute Mediation and Arbitration Agency

Always: Get everything in writing, take before/after photos, and document all communications.

Q: How do I manage pain during recovery?

A: Clinics provide:

  • Prescription pain medication (typically for 3-7 days)
  • Anti-inflammatory drugs
  • Antibiotics to prevent infection
  • Swelling reduction medication

Pain is typically manageable with medication. Most patients report discomfort rather than severe pain.

Q: Can I fly home immediately after surgery?

A: Not recommended. Wait at least:

  • 7-10 days for minor procedures
  • 14 days for moderate procedures
  • 21+ days for major facial contouring

Flying too soon can increase swelling and risks complications due to air pressure changes.
