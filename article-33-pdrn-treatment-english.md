### Q: When is the best time to get PDRN treatment?

**A:** Rejuran is a brand name of high-quality PDRN product from Italy. All Rejuran is PDRN, but not all PDRN is Rejuran. Rejuran typically costs 20-30% more.

### Q: Are results immediate?
