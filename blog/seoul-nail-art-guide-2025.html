What to Expect at Korean Nail Salons

Step-by-Step Process:

  1. Consultation (5 min): Show reference photos or browse salon's portfolio
  2. Nail Prep (10 min): Shaping, cuticle care, buffing
  3. Base Coat (5 min): Apply base gel for durability
  4. Color Application (15-20 min): 2-3 coats of chosen color
  5. Art & Design (10-30 min): If applicable, add nail art
  6. Top Coat & Curing (10 min): Seal with top coat, cure under LED lamp
  7. Hand Massage (5 min): Complimentary hand/arm massage (most salons)

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1. Do Seoul nail salons speak English?

A. Gangnam and tourist areas (Myeongdong) often have English-speaking staff. In Hongdae, communication may be limited but showing photos works well. Book through kbeautyseoul.co.kr to guarantee English support.
