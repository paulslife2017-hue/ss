4. Skin Cycling: Strategic Ingredient Rotation

What is Skin Cycling?

Skin cycling is a methodical approach to using active ingredients in a strategic rotation to maximize benefits while minimizing irritation. Korean dermatologists are leading this trend with scientifically-backed cycling protocols.

  • Night 3-4 - Recovery: Hydration and barrier repair
