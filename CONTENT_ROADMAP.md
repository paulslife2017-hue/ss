## Content Creation Guidelines

### Each Article Must Include:

1. **Word Count**: Minimum 1,800 words, target 2,000-3,000 words
2. **SEO Optimization**:
   - Primary keyword in title, first paragraph, and H2 headers
   - Long-tail keywords throughout (natural placement)
   - Meta description (150-160 characters)
   - Meta keywords (8-12 relevant terms)

3. **Backlinks to kbeautyseoul.co.kr**:
   - Minimum 4 contextual backlinks per article
   - Use varied anchor text ("book online", "instant confirmation", "compare prices", etc.)
   - Include 1-2 CTA boxes with booking links
   - Footer reference link in each article

4. **Structure**:
   - Engaging intro paragraph (150-200 words)
   - Multiple H2 sections (5-8 per article)
   - H3 subsections for detailed breakdowns
   - Comparison tables where relevant
   - FAQ section (5-8 questions)
   - Conclusion with CTA

5. **Images**:
   - Featured image (high-quality, relevant)
   - Placeholder: Use Unsplash URLs related to Korean beauty

6. **Internal Linking**:
   - Link to 2-3 other blog posts
   - Link to category pages
   - Link to homepage
