## How Botox Works: Muscle Relaxation for Wrinkle Reduction

**Botox (Botulinum Toxin Type A)** is a neurotoxin that temporarily paralyzes muscles:

### 1. Blocks Nerve Signals
- Prevents acetylcholine release at nerve endings
- Muscles cannot contract fully
- Expression lines are smoothed

### 2. Immediate Mechanical Effect
- Wrinkles caused by muscle movement disappear
- Skin appears smoother in treated areas
- Effect is visible within days

### 3. Prevents Deepening of Lines
- Muscles stay relaxed for 3-4 months
- Existing wrinkles don't worsen
- Can prevent new wrinkles in treated areas

**Botox Benefits:**
- ‚úÖ Fast, dramatic results
- ‚úÖ Proven track record (30+ years)

- ‚úÖ Minimal downtime
